Professional Summary

Innovative digital learning strategist and senior technology leader with over 15 years of experience bridging human systems, immersive technologies, and educational transformation.

Currently spearheading the development of the “Immersive Global Sim Lab” at Thunderbird School of Global Management—an interdisciplinary learning environment exploring cognitive engagement and human–system interaction through immersive projection and spatial computing.

Seeks to expand this work through action research within the leadership and innovation EdD program at ASU, focusing on AI-driven simulation-based learning and how it can help students navigate complex, global issues.
